Our Tool

This website is designed to illustrate the drug user behavioral pattern based on different drug usage and personality score. Our tool is designed for the young generation and guardians who might get some insights about the drug users' personality and how it might be related to a certain drug usage. In our website, we visualize personality scores of male and female and its correlation with soft drugs and hard drugs. The purpose of this project is to examine the patterns and behaviors of individuals who use soft and hard drugs.

Hard vs Soft Drugs

Hard Drugs: The risks associated with hard drugs are greater than in the case of soft drugs, especially in terms of health hazards, addiction, and the impact on public order. Hard drugs include, for instance, cocaine, and amphetamine.

Soft Drugs: Soft drugs are less hazardous to health than hard drugs. Soft drugs are not harmless substances, but the risks are less serious than the risks associated with using hard drugs. Soft drugs include, alcohol and marijuana.

Limitation

Dataset Description

We are using data from the UCI Drug Consumption Dataset, which includes information about Drug Use and other relevant factors for a large number of respondents.The dataset contains different drugs name,the time of usage,personality scores of the respondents, country, education background and gender.
